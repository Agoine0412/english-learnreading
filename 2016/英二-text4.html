千禧一代的成功观2016 考研英语（二）· Text 4

Younger Americans are drawing a new 21st-century road map to success

难度 考研级词汇·语法·结构 主题 · 代际差异长难句 4
Paragraph 1 — 年轻一代规划新的成功路线图
1
Against a backdrop of drastic changes in economy and population structure, younger Americans are drawing a new 21st-century road map to success, a latest poll has found.
一项最新民调发现，在经济和人口结构剧烈变化的背景下，较年轻的美国人正在绘制一幅崭新的 21 世纪成功路线图。
🔍 语法解析
主干：younger Americans are drawing a new road map to success（主+谓+宾）。
独立主格/状语：Against a backdrop of ...（在……背景下），引出背景信息。
同位语：a latest poll has found → 补充说明信息来源。
词义：backdrop 背景；drastic 剧烈的；poll 民意调查。
Paragraph 2 — 代际之间目标一致，路径不同
2
Across generational lines, Americans continue to prize many of the same traditional milestones of a successful life, including getting married, having children, owning a home, and retiring in their sixties.
跨越代际，美国人仍然珍视许多相同的传统成功人生里程碑——包括结婚、生子、拥有住房和 60 多岁退休。
🔍 语法解析
主干：Americans prize many of the same traditional milestones（主+谓+宾）。
列举：including getting married, having children, owning a home, and retiring → 四个动名词短语并列。
词义：prize 珍视（动词）；milestone 里程碑。
3
But while young and old mostly agree on what constitutes the finish line of a fulfilling life, they offer strikingly different paths for reaching it.
但是，虽然年轻人和老年人大体同意什么构成充实生活的终点线，但他们提出了截然不同的达到终点的方式。
🔍 语法解析
让步状语从句：while young and old mostly agree on what constitutes the finish line（what 引导宾语从句）。
主句：they offer strikingly different paths。
词义：strikingly 显著地/惊人地。
Paragraph 3 — 年轻一代的价值观差异
4
Young people who are still getting started in life were more likely than older adults to prioritize personal fulfillment in their work, to believe they will advance their careers most by regularly changing jobs, to favor communities with more public services and a faster pace of life, to agree that couples should be financially secure before getting married or having children, and to maintain that children are best served by two parents working outside the home, the survey found.
调查发现，仍处于人生起步阶段的年轻人比老年人更可能：优先考虑工作中的个人成就感；认为定期换工作最能推进职业发展；青睐公共服务更多、生活节奏更快的社区；认为夫妻在结婚或生子前应有经济保障；主张父母双方都在外工作最有利于孩子。
🔍 长难句拆解
主干：Young people ... were more likely ... to prioritize ... , to believe ... , to favor ... , to agree ... , and to maintain ...（五个并列不定式短语的复杂结构）。
定语从句：who are still getting started in life → 修饰 young people。
比较结构：more likely than older adults。
五个 to 不定式：每个都是一个独立的观点，列举年轻人的五种不同态度。
  ① to prioritize personal fulfillment in their work
  ② to believe they will advance their careers most by regularly changing jobs
  ③ to favor communities with more public services and a faster pace of life
  ④ to agree that couples should be financially secure before getting married or having children
  ⑤ to maintain that children are best served by two parents working outside the home
💡 Q37 考点：第四个 to (agree that couples should be financially secure before marriage) 对应 C 选项 "attach importance to pre-marital finance"。
词义：prioritize 优先考虑；fulfillment 满足感；financially secure 经济上有保障的。
Paragraph 4 — 这些差异将影响美国生活的方方面面
5
From career to community and family, these contrasts suggest that in the aftermath of the searing Great Recession, those just starting out in life are defining priorities and expectations that will increasingly spread through virtually all aspects of American life, from consumer preferences to housing patterns to politics.
从职业到社区再到家庭，这些对比表明，在灼痛的大衰退之后，那些刚步入人生的人正在定义的优先事项和期望将日益渗透到美国生活的几乎所有方面——从消费偏好到住房模式再到政治。
🔍 长难句拆解
主干：these contrasts suggest that ...（主+谓+宾语从句）。
宾语从句：those ... are defining priorities and expectations that will ... spread through ...
  ① those just starting out in life → 现在分词短语后置定语
  ② that will increasingly spread through virtually all aspects → 定语从句修饰 priorities and expectations
  ③ from consumer preferences to housing patterns to politics → 列举 affected 的方面
词义：aftermath 后果/余波；searing 灼热的/剧烈的。
Paragraph 5 — 两代人都认为年轻人起步更难
6
Young and old converge on one key point: Overwhelming majorities of both groups said they believe it is harder for young people today to get started in life than it was for earlier generations.
年轻人和老年人在一个关键点上达成一致：两个群体中的绝大多数人都说，他们认为今天的年轻人起步比前几代人更难。
🔍 语法解析
主干：Young and old converge on one key point（主+谓+宾）。
同位语：冒号后解释 key point 的内容。
比较级：harder ... than it was for earlier generations。
词义：converge on 在……上达成一致/趋同；overwhelming majority 绝大多数。
7
While younger people are somewhat more optimistic than their elders about the prospects for those starting out today, big majorities in both groups believe those "just getting started in life" face a tougher climb than earlier generations in reaching such signpost achievements as securing a good-paying job, starting a family, managing debt, and finding affordable housing.
虽然年轻人比长辈对今天起步者的前景稍微乐观一些，但两个群体中的绝大多数都认为，"刚起步的人"在达到找到高薪工作、组建家庭、管理债务和找到可负担住房等标志性成就方面，面临着比前几代人更艰难的攀登。
🔍 长难句拆解
让步状语从句：While younger people are somewhat more optimistic ... about the prospects for those starting out today。
主句：big majorities believe those ... face a tougher climb ...
修饰：in reaching such signpost achievements as ... → 介宾短语，列举具体指标。
词义：somewhat 稍微/有点；signpost 路标/标志性的；good-paying job 高薪工作；affordable housing 可负担住房。
Paragraph 6 — Pete Schneider 的故事
8
Pete Schneider considers the climb tougher today.
Pete Schneider 认为如今的攀登更加艰难。
🔍 语法解析
主干：Pete Schneider considers the climb tougher（主+谓+宾+宾补）。tougher = more difficult，与前文 a tougher climb 呼应。
9
Schneider, a 27-year-old auto technician from the Chicago suburbs, says he struggled to find a job after graduating from college.
Schneider 是一位来自芝加哥郊区的 27 岁汽车技师，他说大学毕业后他很难找到工作。
🔍 语法解析
主干：Schneider says（主+谓）+ （that）he struggled to find a job（宾语从句）。
同位语：a 27-year-old auto technician from the Chicago suburbs 解释 Schneider 的身份。
10
Even now that he is working steadily, he said, "I can't afford to pay my monthly mortgage payments on my own, so I have to rent rooms out to people to make that happen."
即使现在他工作稳定了，他说，"我自己付不起月供，所以不得不把房间租给别人来凑钱。"
🔍 语法解析
让步状语从句：Even now that he is working steadily（即使/尽管现在他工作稳定了）。
直接引语：said 后接直接引语。
词义：mortgage 抵押贷款；rent out 出租。
11
Looking back, he is stuck that his parents could provide a comfortable life for their children even though neither had completed college when he was young.
回顾过去，他感慨他的父母——尽管两人年轻时都没上完大学——却能为孩子们提供舒适的生活。
🔍 语法解析
主干：he is stuck that ...（主+系+表+表语从句？这里 stuck 是形容词 + that 从句）。
让步状语从句：even though neither had completed college。
词义：stuck 被触动/感慨（此处熟词生义）。
12
"I still grew up in an upper middle-class home with parents who didn't have college degrees," Schneider said. "I don't think people are capable of that anymore."
"我仍然在一个中上阶级家庭长大，父母都没有大学学位，"Schneider 说。"我认为人们再也做不到那样了。"
🔍 长难句拆解
第一句：I grew up in an upper middle-class home + with parents who didn't have college degrees（定语从句修饰 parents）。
第二句：I don't think people are capable of that anymore（否定转移：I don't think = "我认为……不"）。
💡 Q40 考点：Schneider 父母没有大学学位却提供了中上阶级的生活。说明好生活与大学学位关系不大。

📋 全文脉络 ▲ 收起

① P1: 年轻美国人正在绘制新的成功路线图。
② P2: 代际间人生目标一致（结婚生子买房退休），但实现路径有别。
③ P3: 年轻人更重视个人成就感、频繁换工作、婚前经济保障等。
④ P4: 这些新价值观将渗透到美国生活的所有方面。
⑤ P5: 两代人都认为年轻人起步比前几代人更难。
⑥ P6: Schneider 的故事——大学毕业后难找工作，房贷压力大，父母无学位却生活舒适。

📝 题目答案速查 ▼ 展开

36. One cross-generation mark of a successful life is ______.
[A] trying out different lifestyles [B] having a family with children [C] working beyond retirement age [D] setting up a profitable business
参考答案：B

✅ 正确答案：B（having a family with children 组建家庭生儿育女）

定位：P2 · S02 — "Americans continue to prize many of the same traditional milestones of a successful life, including getting married, having children, owning a home, and retiring in their sixties."

推理：作者列举了跨代际共识的传统成功人生里程碑——结婚、生子、买房、退休。B 选项 "having a family with children"（组建家庭有孩子）对应原文的 "getting married, having children"。

❌ A 错误：trying out different lifestyles（尝试不同的生活方式）——文中列举的跨代际共识都是传统价值观（结婚生子买房退休），不是"尝试不同生活方式"。

❌ C 错误：working beyond retirement age（退休后继续工作）——与原文 "retiring in their sixties"（60 多岁退休）相反。

❌ D 错误：setting up a profitable business（创办盈利企业）——文中未提及创业是跨代际共识。

37. It can be learned from Paragraph 3 that young people tend to ______.
[A] favor a slower life pace [B] hold an occupation longer [C] attach importance to pre-marital finance [D] give priority to childcare outside the home
参考答案：C

✅ 正确答案：C（attach importance to pre-marital finance 重视婚前经济状况）

定位：P3 · S04 — 五个不定式中的第四个："to agree that couples should be financially secure before getting married or having children"

推理：年轻人认为夫妻在结婚或生子前应有经济保障（financially secure）= attach importance to pre-marital finance（重视婚前经济状况）。C 选项是原文的同义转述。

❌ A 错误：favor a slower life pace（青睐较慢的生活节奏）——与原文相反！S04 说年轻人 "favor communities with ... a faster pace of life"（更快的生活节奏）。

❌ B 错误：hold an occupation longer（更长时间从事一份职业）——与原文相反！S04 说年轻人 "believe they will advance their careers most by regularly changing jobs"（定期换工作）。

❌ D 错误：give priority to childcare outside the home（优先考虑家庭外的儿童看护）——S04 说 "children are best served by two parents working outside the home"（父母都外出工作最有利于孩子），但这说的是"双职工"而非"家庭外儿童看护"。

38. The priorities and expectations defined by the young will ______.
[A] become increasingly clear [B] focus on materialistic issues [C] depend largely on political preferences [D] reach almost all aspects of American life
参考答案：D

✅ 正确答案：D（reach almost all aspects of American life 触及美国生活的几乎所有方面）

定位：P4 · S05 — "priorities and expectations that will increasingly spread through virtually all aspects of American life, from consumer preferences to housing patterns to politics"

推理：年轻人的优先事项和期望将日益渗透到美国生活的几乎所有方面。D 选项 "reach almost all aspects" 是 "spread through virtually all aspects" 的同义替换。原文还用 "from ... to ... to ..." 具体列举了涉及的方面。

❌ A 错误：become increasingly clear（变得越来越清晰）——原文强调范围（spread through all aspects），而非清晰度。

❌ B 错误：focus on materialistic issues（聚焦物质问题）——虽然消费者偏好和住房涉及物质层面，但还涉及政治等非物质层面，不能一概而论。

❌ C 错误：depend largely on political preferences（很大程度上取决于政治偏好）——文中说 priorities 将影响政治（to politics），而不是被政治影响。

39. Both young and old agree that ______.
[A] good-paying jobs are less available [B] the old made more life achievements [C] housing loans today are easy to obtain [D] getting established is harder for the young
参考答案：D

✅ 正确答案：D（getting established is harder for the young 年轻人立足更难）

定位：P5 · S06-S07 — "Overwhelming majorities of both groups said they believe it is harder for young people today to get started in life than it was for earlier generations" + "face a tougher climb than earlier generations"

推理：两代人的共识是：年轻人起步比前辈更难（harder to get started / tougher climb）。D 选项 getting established is harder for the young（年轻人立足更难）准确概括了这一共识。

❌ A 错误：good-paying jobs are less available（高薪工作更难找到）——文中提到 "securing a good-paying job" 是更难达到的成就之一，但这不是两代人共识的核心表述。核心是"整体起步更难"。

❌ B 错误：the old made more life achievements（老年人取得了更多人生成就）——文中说的是年轻人面临更艰难的攀登，并不是说老年人成就更多。

❌ C 错误：housing loans today are easy to obtain（如今住房贷款容易获得）——与文意相反！Schneider 的例子正说明贷款负担重，且 "affordable housing" 是年轻人面临的困难之一。

40. Which of the following is true about Schneider?
[A] He found a dream job after graduating from college. [B] His parents believe working steadily is a must for success. [C] His parents' good life has little to do with a college degree. [D] He thinks his job as a technician quite challenging.
参考答案：C

✅ 正确答案：C（His parents' good life has little to do with a college degree 他父母的好生活与大学学位关系不大）

定位：P6 · S11-S12 — "neither had completed college when he was young ... I still grew up in an upper middle-class home with parents who didn't have college degrees ... I don't think people are capable of that anymore."

推理：Schneider 的父母虽未完成大学学业，却为他提供了中上阶级的生活。这说明父母的好生活与大学学位关系不大。且 Schneider 感慨"现在的人做不到那样了"——进一步印证了他认为父母那一代不需要大学学位也能过得很好。

❌ A 错误：found a dream job after graduating（毕业后找到了梦想的工作）——S09 说 "he struggled to find a job after graduating from college"（毕业后很难找到工作），dream job 更无从谈起。

❌ B 错误：His parents believe working steadily is a must（他父母认为稳定工作是成功的必要条件）——文中说的是 Schneider 本人 "working steadily"，而非他父母的看法。

❌ D 错误：thinks his job as a technician quite challenging（认为他的技师工作很有挑战性）——文中未提及他对工作难度的评价。
