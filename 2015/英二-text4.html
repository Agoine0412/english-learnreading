就业市场：被忽视的兼职真相2015 考研英语（二）· Text 4

从28.8万新增岗位到奥巴马医改——就业数据背后隐藏的"自愿兼职"之谜

难度 ★★★☆ 词汇量 338 主题 · 劳动经济 长难句 6
01
Many people talked of the 288,000 new jobs the Labor Department reported for June, along with the drop in the unemployment rate to 6.1 percent, as good news.
许多人都在谈论劳工部报告的6月份28.8万新增岗位以及失业率降至6.1%，认为这是好消息。
🔍 长难句拆解
主语 = Many people（许多人）
谓语 = talked of... as good news（把……当作好消息来谈论）—talk of A as B = 把A称作/视为B。
宾语（超长名词短语）：
  • 核心名词 = the 288,000 new jobs（28.8万新工作岗位）
  • 定语从句（省略that）= (that) the Labor Department reported for June（劳工部报告的6月份数据）
  • 伴随状语 = along with the drop in the unemployment rate to 6.1 percent（以及失业率下降到6.1%）
💡 句式亮点："along with..." 并列补充第二个积极指标（失业率下降），与"new jobs"共同构成"好消息"的证据。
02
And they were right.
他们的看法没错。
🔍 语法解析
① 简单主系表结构：And they were right — And 承接上文；they 指代 Many people；right 是形容词作表语，表示"对的/正确的"。极简短句起承上启下作用——先承认表面数据的积极面，为下文 However 转折做铺垫。
② 写作技巧：先肯定再转折（"他们说得对，但是……"），是考研阅读常见的论证套路。
03
For now it appears the economy is creating jobs at a decent pace.
目前看来，经济正在以不错的速度创造就业岗位。
🔍 语法解析
① For now 作时间状语 = 目前/就现在而言，限定结论的时效性（暗示后续可能变化）。
② it appears (that) = 看起来/似乎，that 省略，后接宾语从句 the economy is creating jobs at a decent pace（经济正以相当不错的速度在创造就业机会）。it appears 表示作者谨慎态度——数据看起来好，但未必全貌如此。
③ decent pace = 体面的速度/还不错的节奏；decent 原意"得体的、像样的"，此处形容经济增速"尚可"。
04
We still have a long way to go to get back to full employment, but at least we are now finally moving forward at a faster pace.
我们距离恢复充分就业还有很长的路要走，但至少我们终于开始以更快的速度前进了。
🔍 语法解析
① but 并列转折句：前半（消极）We still have a long way to... vs 后半（积极）at least we are now finally... — 先抑后扬。
② have a long way to go to do sth = 要做某事还有很长的路要走/还需努力。
③ full employment = 充分就业（经济学术语，指愿意工作的人都有工作的状态）。
④ at least = 至少；finally / faster pace 强调进步加速。
⑤ moving forward at a faster pace = 以更快速度向前推进 — forward 方向副词修饰 move。
05
However, there is another important part of the jobs picture that was largely overlooked.
然而，就业图景中还有一个重要部分在很大程度上被忽视了。
🔍 长难句拆解
There be 句型：there is ... part（有……一部分）
主语：another important part of the jobs picture（就业图景的另一个重要部分）—jobs picture = 就业形势/就业全景图
定语从句：that was largely overlooked（很大程度上被忽视的）—that 修饰 part；overlook = 忽视/忽略（及物动词，被动语态）
💡 逻辑枢纽：However 标志全文重大转折点——前面四句讲"就业数据看起来不错"，从此句开始揭示"被忽视的问题"。这是 Q36 的定位句！
06
There was a big jump in the number of people who report voluntarily working part-time.
报告自愿从事兼职工作的人数出现了大幅增长。
🔍 语法解析
① There be 句型：There was a big jump in...（……出现了大幅增长/跳跃）
② 介词短语限定范围：in the number of people who...（在……的人群数量方面）
③ who 定语从句：who report voluntarily working part-time（那些报告自愿做兼职的人）—who 修饰 people；report 后接动名词 working 作宾语；voluntarily 副词修饰 working。
重点词：voluntary /ˈvɒləntri/ adj. 自愿的 ↔ involuntary（非自愿的）；part-time n./adj. 兼职（的）↔ full-time 全职。
07
This figure is now 830,000 (4.4 percent) above its year ago level.
这个数字如今比一年前水平高出83万人（增幅4.4%）。
🔍 语法解析
① 主系表结构：This figure is now 830,000 ... above its year ago level — This figure 回指 S06 的"自愿兼职人数的大幅增长"
② above = 高于/超出……（介词）；year ago level = 一年前的水平/基数
③ 数据解读：830,000人 / 4.4% ——两个数字互相印证（83万占整体劳动力的4.4%），强调增幅显著。
08
Before explaining the connection to the Obamacare, it is worth making an important distinction.
在解释其与奥巴马医改的联系之前，有必要先做一个重要区分。
🔍 语法解析
① Before doing 作时间状语：Before explaining the connection to the Obamacare = 在解释与奥巴马医改的关联之前
② it is worth doing sth = 值得做某事（worth 后接动名词 making）
③ make a distinction = 做/做出区分/区别
④ Obamacare = Obama + Care 的合成词，指《患者保护与平价医疗法案》（Affordable Care Act），俗称"奥巴马医改"
💡 结构功能：典型的过渡句——预告下文将讨论"自愿兼职与医改的关系"，但先铺垫一个概念辨析。
09
Many people who work part-time jobs actually want full-time jobs.
许多做兼职工作的人实际上想要全职工作。
🔍 语法解析
① who 定语从句：who work part-time jobs（做兼职工作的人）—who 修饰 Many people
② actually = 实际上/事实上（副词，加强语气，暗示"与直觉相反"）
③ 核心对比：part-time jobs（兼职）vs full-time jobs（全职）—本文的核心概念对子
10
They take part-time work because this is all they can get.
他们接受兼职工作是因为这是他们能得到的全部。
🔍 语法解析
① because 引导原因状语从句：because this is all they can get（因为他们只能得到这些/别无选择）
② 表语从句（省略that）：this is all (that) they can get — all 是先行词，they can get 是省略 that 的定语从句修饰 all。all (that) they can get = 他们所能得到的一切
💡 含义：这句话揭示的是 非自愿兼职（involuntary part-time）的本质——不是不想全职，而是找不到全职。"this is all they can get" 是非常口语化但有力的表达。
11
An increase in involuntary part-time work is evidence of weakness in the labor market and it means that many people will be having a very hard time making ends meet.
非自愿兼职的增加是劳动力市场疲软的证据，这意味着许多人将难以维持生计。
🔍 长难句拆解
并列主语 + 双谓语：An increase... is evidence... and it means... —and 连接两个分句：
  分句一：An increase in involuntary part-time work is evidence of weakness in the labor market
  • An increase in... = ……的增加（介词短语作主语）
  • involuntary part-time work = 非自愿兼职工作
  • is evidence of weakness = 是……疲软的证据（evidence 后接 of 短语说明证据指向什么）
  分句二：it means that many people will be having a very hard time making ends meet
  • it = 前半句提到的现象
  • means that + 从句 = 意味着……
  • have a hard time (in) doing sth = 做某事很吃力/困难
  • make ends meet = 勉强维持生计/收支相抵（固定习语）
💡 逻辑链条：非自愿兼职↑ → 劳动力市场弱 → 人们生计艰难（Q37 的核心依据）
12
There was an increase in involuntary part-time in June, but the general direction has been down.
6月份非自愿兼职有所增加，但总体趋势是在下降。
🔍 语法解析
① but 转折：There was an increase... but the general direction has been down — 单月增加 vs 整体趋势下降，体现数据的复杂性。
② general direction has been down = 总体方向一直在下行/呈下降趋势（has been 表示持续到现在）
③ down = 向下的/减少的（形容词，作表语）
13
Involuntary part-time employment is still far higher than before the recession, but it is down by 640,000 (7.9 percent) from its year ago level.
非自愿兼职就业仍远高于衰退前的水平，但比一年前减少了64万人（降幅7.9%）。
🔍 长难句拆解
but 连接的两个对比分句：
  分句一（坏消息）：Involuntary part-time employment is still far higher than before the recession
  • Involuntary part-time employment = 非自愿兼职就业（完整术语）
  • far higher than = 远高于（far 修饰比较级 higher）
  • before the recession = 经济衰退之前（recession = 经济衰退）
  分句二（好消息）：it is down by 640,000 (7.9 percent) from its year ago level
  • it = Involuntary part-time employment
  • down by 640,000 = 减少了64万（by 表示幅度）
  • from its year ago level = 与一年前的水平相比
💡 数据逻辑：仍高于衰退前 → 问题未解决；但比去年降了64万 → 在改善。Q38 的答案依据在此句。
14
We know the difference between voluntary and involuntary part-time employment because people tell us.
我们能区分自愿和非自愿兼职就业，因为人们会告诉我们。
🔍 语法解析
① because 引导原因状语从句：because people tell us（因为人们告诉了我们）—tell us 指调查受访者如实回答问卷。
② the difference between A and B = A 和 B 之间的区别
💡 过渡功能：此句引出下文对"如何区分"的具体解释（劳工部调查方法）。
15
The survey used by the Labor Department asks people if they worked less than 35 hours in the reference week.
劳工部使用的调查会询问人们在参考周内是否工作了不到35小时。
🔍 语法解析
① 过去分词作后置定语：(The survey) used by the Labor Department = 劳工部使用的（调查）—used by... 修饰 survey
② if 宾语从句：asks people if they worked less than 35 hours... —if 引导的间接问句，asks 的直接宾语
③ in the reference week = 在参考周内（统计学术语，指调查所覆盖的那一周）
④ less than = 少于（比较级结构）
16
If the answer is "yes", they are classified as working part-time.
如果回答"是"，他们就被归类为兼职工作者。
🔍 语法解析
① If 条件句：If the answer is "yes"（如果回答是"是的"）—真实条件句
② 被动语态：they are classified as working part-time（他们被归类为兼职工作者）—classify A as B = 把A归类为B
17
The survey then asks whether they worked less than 35 hours in that week because they wanted to work less than full time or because they had no choice.
接着调查询问他们在那周工作时间不足35小时，是因为想要少于全职的工作时长，还是因为没有选择。
🔍 长难句拆解
主句谓语 + whether 宾语从句：The survey then asks whether...（然后调查询问是否……）
whether 从句内部：they worked less than 35 hours in that week because A or because B
  • 核心事实 = they worked less than 35 hours in that week（那周工作了不到35小时）
  • 原因① = because they wanted to work less than full time（因为想少干点）→ 对应 **voluntary（自愿）**
  • 原因② = because they had no choice（因为没有选择）→ 对应 **involuntary（非自愿）**
  • or 连接两个原因，构成排他性二选一
💡 关键区分机制：这就是"自愿"与"非自愿"的操作定义——同一行为（<35h），不同动机 → 不同分类。
18
They are only classified as voluntary part-time workers if they tell the survey taker they chose to work less than 35 hours a week.
只有当受访者告诉调查员是他们自己选择每周工作不到35小时时，才会被归为自愿兼职者。
🔍 语法解析
① only if 条件句：only if... = 只有当……才……（比 if 更强的条件限制）
② 被动语态：are classified as... = 被归类为……
③ 嵌套宾语从句链：tell [the survey taker] [they chose to work less than 35 hours a week]
  • tell sb (that) ... = 告诉某人……（that 省略）
  • they chose to do sth = 他们选择了做某事（choose to do = 选择去做）
💡 与S17呼应：S17 说"两种原因"，S18 明确"只有选第一种才叫 voluntary"——强化分类标准。
19
The issue of voluntary part-time relates to Obamacare because one of the main purposes was to allow people to get insurance outside of employment.
自愿兼职问题与奥巴马医改有关联，因为其主要目的之一就是让人们能够不通过就业渠道获得保险。
🔍 语法解析
① 主语：The issue of voluntary part-time = 自愿兼职这个问题
② relate to = 与……有关/相关联
③ because 原因状语从句：because one of the main purposes was to allow people to get insurance outside of employment
  • one of the main purposes = 主要目的之一
  • was to allow... = 是为了允许……（不定式 to allow 作表语，说明 purpose 的具体内容）
  • get insurance outside of employment = 在就业之外获得保险（outside of = 在……之外）
💡 核心论点：这句话建立全文最重要的因果链条——Obamacare 切断了"就业→保险"的绑定关系→ 影响人们的就业选择。
20
For many people, especially those with serious health conditions or family members with serious health conditions, before Obamacare the only way to get insurance was through a job that provided health insurance.
对于许多人来说——尤其是那些本人或家人患有严重健康问题的人——在奥巴马医改之前，获得保险的唯一途径是通过一份提供医疗保险的工作。
🔍 长难句拆解
主句框架：For many people, ... the only way to get insurance was through a job...
插入语（逗号隔离）：especially those with serious health conditions or family members with serious health conditions
  • especially = 尤其是
  • those = 那些（人），后接两个 with 介词短语并行：
   - those with serious health conditions = 本人有严重健康问题的（人）
   - (those) family members with serious health conditions = 家人有严重健康问题的（人）（those 省略，family members 共享 with）
时间状语：before Obamacare = 在奥巴马医改之前
主句核心：the only way to get insurance was through a job that provided health insurance
  • the only way to do sth = 做某事的唯一途径
  • through a job = 通过一份工作
  • that provided health insurance = 提供医疗保险的（that 定语从句修饰 job）
💡 论证力度：用"唯一途径（only way）"+ 具体人群（重症患者）强化 Obamcare 之前的困境——为了保险被迫找全职工作，即使身体条件不允许或只需要兼职。
21
However, Obamacare has allowed more than 12 million people to either get insurance through Medicaid or the exchanges.
然而，奥巴马医改已经使超过1200万人能够通过医疗补助计划或保险交易市场获得保险。
🔍 语法解析
① however 转折：However, Obamacare has allowed... — 但医改已经让……（转折前文"以前只能靠工作"）
② allow sb to do sth = 使某人能够做某事
③ either... or...：either get insurance through Medicaid or the exchanges = 要么通过医疗补助计划(Medicaid)，要么通过保险交易市场(the exchanges)获得保险
重点词：Medicaid /ˈmedɪkeɪd/ n. 医疗补助计划（美国面向低收入者的公共医保项目）；exchanges 此处 = 医保交易所/交易市场（Obamacare 建立的在线保险购买平台）
22
These are people who may previously have felt the need to get a full-time job that provided insurance in order to cover themselves and their families.
这些人此前可能觉得有必要找一份提供保险的全职工作，以便为自己和家人提供保障。
🔍 语法解析
① These 指代：These = more than 12 million people（上句的1200多万人）
② who 定语从句：who may previously have felt the need to get a full-time job...（他们此前可能觉得需要找一份全职工作……）—may previously have felt = 可能此前一直觉得（may + 完成时态表示对过去情况的推测）
③ feel the need to do sth = 觉得有必要/需要做某事
④ 不定式的目的嵌套：get a full-time job [that provided insurance] in order to cover themselves and their families
  • that provided insurance = 提供保险的（定语从句修饰 job）
  • in order to cover = 以便保障/覆盖（cover 此处 = 支付……的费用/提供保障）
  • themselves and their families = 自己和家人
💡 逻辑关键：previously（以前）vs now（现在有了Obamacare可以不走这条路的对比隐含其中。
23
With Obamacare there is no longer a link between employment and insurance.
随着奥巴马医改的实施，就业与保险之间不再存在关联。
🔍 语法解析
① With + 名词：With Obamacare = 随着/有了奥巴马医改（with 表伴随情况/条件）
② no longer = 不再
③ a link between A and B = A和B之间的联系/关联
💡 全文结论句：这句话是整篇文章的落脚点——Obamacare 打破了传统的"就业=保险"捆绑模式，这解释了为什么自愿兼职人数大增（S06-S07）：人们不再为了保险而被迫找全职工作了。Q39和Q40的核心依据均源于此句及其上下文。

📋 全文脉络 ▲ 收起

  • ① 现象提出（P1，S01-S04）：6月就业数据看似亮眼（28.8万新岗位+失业率降至6.1%），经济在以不错速度复苏，但距充分就业仍有距离。
  • ② 转折引入（P2，S05-S07）：However——就业图景中有一个被严重忽视的部分：自愿兼职人数激增83万（+4.4%）。
  • ③ 概念辨析（P3，S08-S11）：先区分两类兼职——许多人做兼职并非出于自愿（actually want full-time），而是"只能得到这些"（非自愿兼职=劳动力市场疲软的证据=生计艰难）。
  • ④ 数据呈现（P4，S12-S13）：6月非自愿兼职微增，但整体趋势下降（同比-64万/-7.9%）；不过仍远高于衰退前水平。
  • ⑤ 分类方法论（P5，S14-S18）：解释如何通过劳工部调查区分"自愿"vs"非自愿"——<35小时/周的相同行为，因动机不同（主动选择vs没得选）而归入不同类别。
  • ⑥ 因果关联（P6，S19-S20）：自愿兼职激增与Obamacare相关——医改前，重症患者为了获得保险只能找带医保的全职工作（唯一途径）。
  • ⑦ 结论收尾（P7，S21-S23）：Obamacare已使1200多万人通过Medicaid/交易市场获得保险——这些人不再需要为保险被迫全职→就业与保险的绑定已被切断（全文核心论点）。

36 Which part of the jobs picture was neglected?
[A] The prospect of a thriving job market. [B] The increase of voluntary part-time jobs. [C] The possibility of full employment. [D] The acceleration of job creation.

🔍 定位：P2 S05 "However, there is another important part of the jobs picture that was largely overlooked." + S06 "There was a big jump in the number of people who report voluntarily working part-time."

✅ 正确答案推理（B）：S05 明确指出有一个"重要部分被大大忽视（largely overlooked）"，紧接着 S06 揭示这个被忽视的部分就是"自愿兼职人数大幅增长（big jump... voluntarily working part-time）"。neglected ≈ overlooked，答案锁定 B。

❌ A 选项（繁荣就业市场前景）：这正是文章开头 P1 所讨论的"好消息"部分（28.8万新岗位+失业率降），是被大家广泛讨论的，不是被"忽视"的部分。

❌ C 选项（充分就业的可能性）：S04 提到 "full employment"，说的是"离充分就业还很远"，属于已知困难，不是被忽视的新发现。

❌ D 选项（就业创造的加速）：S03-S04 讨论的正是就业创造的加速（moving forward at a faster pace），同样不是被忽视的内容。

37 Many people work part-time because they _______.
[A] prefer part-time jobs to full-time jobs [B] feel that is enough to make ends meet [C] cannot get their hands on full-time jobs [D] haven't seen the weakness of the market

🔍 定位：P3 S09-S10 — "Many people who work part-time jobs actually want full-time jobs. They take part-time work because this is all they can get."

✅ 正确答案推理（C）：S09 明确说兼职的人"actually want full-time jobs"（其实想要全职），S10 解释原因"this is all they can get"（这是他们能得到的全部/别无选择）。cannot get their hands on full-time jobs = 无法获得全职工作 = "this is all they can get"的同义替换。

❌ A 选项（更喜欢兼职而非全职）：正好与原文相反！S09 用 actually 强调"他们其实是想要全职的"——A 描述的是"自愿兼职"的特征，而 S09-S10 讨论的恰恰是非自愿兼职者。

❌ B 选项（觉得足够维持生计）：与 S11 矛盾——S11 说非自愿兼职意味着"many people will be having a very hard time making ends meet"（很多人将很难维持生计），而不是"足够"。

❌ D 选项（还没看到市场的弱点）：S11 明确指出 "An increase in involuntary part-time work is evidence of weakness in the labor market"——兼职者正是在经历市场弱点（找不到全职），而非"没看到"。

38 Involuntary part-time employment in the US _______.
[A] is harder to acquire than one year ago [B] shows a general tendency of decline [C] satisfies the real need of the jobless [D] is lower than before the recession

🔍 定位：P4 S12-S13 — "There was an increase in involuntary part-time in June, but the general direction has been down. Involuntary part-time employment is still far higher than before the recession, but it is down by 640,000 (7.9 percent) from its year ago level."

✅ 正确答案推理（B）：S12 明确说 "the general direction has been down"（总体趋势是向下的/下降的），S13 补充数据支撑：同比减少64万（-7.9%）。shows a general tendency of decline = 总体呈下降趋势，完美对应。

❌ A 选项（比一年前更难获得）：acquire（获取/获得）用词不当——非自愿兼职不是人们"想要获取的东西"，而是被迫接受的状况。而且原文说它在"下降"（down），不是变得更难。

❌ C 选项（满足失业者的真实需求）：S11 明确指出非自愿兼职 = "having a very hard time making ends meet"（生计艰难），显然不"满足需求"。只有自愿（找到理想兼职）才可能接近"满足需求"。

❌ D 选项（低于衰退前水平）：原文 S13 说 "still far higher than before the recession"（仍然远高于衰退前水平）—still far higher ≠ lower，D 与原文直接矛盾。

39 It can be learned that with Obamacare, _______.
[A] it is no longer easy for part-timers to get insurance [B] employment is no longer a precondition to get insurance [C] it is still challenging to get insurance for family members [D] full-time employment is still essential for insurance
