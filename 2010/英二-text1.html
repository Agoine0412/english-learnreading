艺术市场的衰退 2010 英二 · Text 1

The longest bull run in a century of art-market history — 逐句精读 · 词汇 · 语法 · 命题分析
难度 考研级 词汇·语法·结构 逐句解析 19 句 · 5 题
📊 难度推断：考研级。本文含艺术市场/金融相关词汇（bull run, auction, bankruptcy, momentum, recession 等），含同位语结构、非限制性定语从句、插入语等考研核心语法。
Paragraph 1 — 艺术市场百年最长牛市以戏剧性方式终结
S01
The longest bull run in a century of art-market history ended on a dramatic note with a sale of 56 works by Damien Hirst, Beautiful Inside My Head Forever, at Sotheby's in London on September 15th, 2008.
译文
2008 年 9 月 15 日，伦敦苏富比拍卖行以达明安·赫斯特 56 件作品的拍卖《我脑海中永恒的美》戏剧性地结束了艺术市场史上持续百年的最长牛市。
▶ 语法分析 长难句
主干：The longest bull run ... ended on a dramatic note with a sale ...（简单句，含多组修饰语）
修饰：in a century of art-market history → 后置定语；with a sale ... → 伴随状语，说明以何种方式结束；Beautiful Inside My Head Forever → 同位语解释 sale 的名称
词义：bull run 牛市（金融市场术语，熊市=bear market）· on a dramatic note 以戏剧性的方式 · fetch 售得（多少钱）
S02
All but two pieces sold, fetching more than £70m, a record for a sale by a single artist.
译文
除两件外全部售出，成交额超过 7000 万英镑，创下了单名艺术家的拍卖纪录。
▶ 语法分析
主干：All but two pieces sold（简单句，but = except「除了」）
修饰：fetching ... → 现在分词作结果状语；a record ... → 名词短语作同位语
词义：all but 除…之外全部 · fetch 售得（多少钱）
S03
It was a last victory.
译文
这是最后的胜利。
▶ 语法分析
结构：简单句，主系表。last 意为"最后"。
S04
As the auctioneer called out bids, in New York one of the oldest banks on Wall Street, Lehman Brothers, filed for bankruptcy.
译文
就在拍卖师喊出报价的同时，在纽约，华尔街历史最悠久的银行之一——雷曼兄弟——申请了破产。
▶ 语法分析 长难句
主干：one of the oldest banks ... filed for bankruptcy（主+谓+宾）
修饰：As the auctioneer called out bids → 时间状语从句（as = 正当…时）；in New York → 地点状语；Lehman Brothers → 同位语解释 bank；on Wall Street → 后置定语
词义：auctioneer 拍卖师 · file for bankruptcy 申请破产
Paragraph 2 — 艺术市场早已失去增长动力
S05
The world art market had already been losing momentum for a while after rising bewilderingly since 2003.
译文
全球艺术市场自 2003 年起令人眩目地飙升了一阵后，早已开始失去增长动力。
▶ 语法分析
主干：The world art market had been losing momentum（过去完成进行时，强调动作持续到过去某时间点）
词义：momentum 动力 / 势头 · bewilderingly 令人眩目地 · for a while 一段时间
S06
At its peak in 2007 it was worth some $65 billion, reckons Clare McAndrew, founder of Arts Economics, a research firm—double the figure five years earlier.
译文
研究公司 Arts Economics 的创始人 Clare McAndrew 估计，在 2007 年的高峰期，它（艺术市场）价值约 650 亿美元——是五年前的两倍。
▶ 语法分析 长难句
主干：it was worth some $65 billion（主系表）+ reckons Clare McAndrew（插入式引述结构）
修饰：founder of Arts Economics, a research firm → 双重同位语（Clare McAndrew = founder = research firm）；破折号后 double the figure → 名词短语补充说明 $65 billion
词义：reckon 估计 · peak 高峰
S07
Since then it may have come down to $50 billion.
译文
自那以后，它可能已降至 500 亿美元。
▶ 语法分析
结构：may have come down（情态动词+完成时，表推测）
S08
But the market generates interest far beyond its size because it brings together great wealth, enormous egos, greed, passion and controversy in a way matched by few other industries.
译文
但该市场引发的关注远远超出了其规模，因为它以一种很少有其他行业能匹敌的方式将巨额财富、巨大的自我、贪婪、激情和争议集于一身。
▶ 语法分析 长难句
主干：the market generates interest + because（原因状语从句）
从句：it brings together ... in a way matched by few other industries → 过去分词短语 matched by ... 作后置定语修饰 way
词义：generate 产生 · ego 自我 / 自负 · controversy 争议 · matched by 与…匹敌
Paragraph 3 — 赫斯特拍卖后，艺术市场急剧萎缩
S09
In the weeks and months that followed Mr. Hirst's sale, spending of any sort became deeply unfashionable.
译文
在赫斯特拍卖之后的数周和数月里，任何形式的支出都变得非常不流行了。
▶ 语法分析
主干：spending ... became deeply unfashionable（主系表）
修饰：that followed Mr. Hirst's sale → 定语从句修饰 weeks and months
词义：unfashionable 不流行的
S10
In the art world that meant collectors stayed away from galleries and salerooms.
译文
在艺术界，这意味着收藏家们远离了画廊和拍卖厅。
▶ 语法分析
主干：that meant（主谓）+（that）collectors stayed away（省略 that 的宾语从句）
词义：stay away from 远离 · saleroom 拍卖厅
S11
Sales of contemporary art fell by two-thirds, and in the most overheated sector, they were down by nearly 90% in the year to November 2008.
译文
当代艺术品的销售额下降了三分之二，而在最过热的领域，截至 2008 年 11 月的一年中，降幅近 90%。
▶ 语法分析
结构：并列句（and 连接两个分句）；fell by two-thirds 和 were down by nearly 90% 形成递进
词义：contemporary art 当代艺术 · overheated 过热的 · sector 领域
S12
Within weeks the world's two biggest auction houses, Sotheby's and Christie's, had to pay out nearly $200m in guarantees to clients who had placed works for sale with them.
译文
数周之内，全球两大拍卖行苏富比和佳士得不得不向委托其拍卖作品的客户支付近 2 亿美元的保证金。
▶ 语法分析
主干：the world's two biggest auction houses ... had to pay out nearly $200m in guarantees（主谓宾）
修饰：Sotheby's and Christie's → 同位语；who had placed works for sale with them → 定语从句修饰 clients
词义：auction house 拍卖行 · guarantee 保证金 · place for sale 委托销售
Paragraph 4 — 当前衰退是自 1989 年以来最严重的
S13
The current downturn in the art market is the worst since the Japanese stopped buying Impressionists at the end of 1989.
译文
当前艺术市场的低迷是自 1989 年底日本人停止购买印象派作品以来最严重的。
▶ 语法分析
主干：The current downturn ... is the worst ... since ...（主系表 + since 时间状语从句）
词义：downturn 衰退 · Impressionist 印象派画家
S14
This time experts reckon that prices are about 40% down on their peak on average, though some have been far more fluctuant.
译文
专家估计，此次价格平均比峰值下跌了约 40%，尽管有些作品的价格波动要大得多。
▶ 语法分析
主干：experts reckon that ...（主谓 + 宾语从句）
从句：prices are about 40% down + though（让步状语从句）
词义：fluctuant 波动的 · on average 平均
S15
But Edward Dolman, Christie's chief executive, says: "I'm pretty confident we're at the bottom."
译文
但佳士得首席执行官 Edward Dolman 说："我很有信心我们已经见底了。"
▶ 语法分析
结构：直接引语作宾语；Christie's chief executive → 同位语
词义：chief executive 首席执行官 · at the bottom 在底部 / 已见底
Paragraph 5 — 此次衰退的不同之处：仍有买家，缺的是好作品
S16
What makes this slump different from the last, he says, is that there are still buyers in the market.
译文
他说，此次衰退与上一次的不同之处在于，市场上仍有买家。
▶ 语法分析 长难句
主干：What makes this slump different from the last（主语从句）+ is + that ...（表语从句）
语法：主语从句 + 表语从句，嵌套结构，考研高频句型
词义：slump 衰退 / 不景气
S17
Almost everyone who was interviewed for this special report said that the biggest problem at the moment is not a lack of demand but a lack of good work to sell.
译文
几乎所有接受本特别报道采访的人都说，目前最大的问题不是缺乏需求，而是缺乏好的作品可卖。
▶ 语法分析 长难句
主干：Almost everyone ... said that ...（主谓 + 宾语从句）
修饰：who was interviewed → 定语从句修饰 everyone；that the biggest problem ... → 宾语从句；not ... but ... 不是…而是…（强调后者）
词义：lack of demand 需求不足 · special report 特别报道
S18
The three Ds—death, debt and divorce—still deliver works of art to the market.
译文
三个 D——死亡、债务和离婚——仍然将艺术品输送到市场。
▶ 语法分析
结构：破折号引出同位语（death, debt and divorce）解释 three Ds
词义：deliver ... to 输送到
S19
But anyone who does not have to sell is keeping away, waiting for confidence to return.
译文
但那些不必出售的人都在观望，等待信心恢复。
▶ 语法分析
主干：anyone ... is keeping away（主谓）
修饰：who does not have to sell → 定语从句修饰 anyone；waiting for ... → 现在分词作伴随状语
词义：keep away 远离 / 观望 · confidence 信心

📋 全文脉络 ▼ 展开

①P1: 标志性事件——赫斯特的拍卖以 7000 万英镑收场，苏富比拍卖师喊价的同时雷曼兄弟申请破产，艺术市场百年牛市就此终结。
②P2: 背景——艺术市场自 2003 年飙升后已开始失去动力，2007 年高峰达 6500 亿美元，虽降至 5000 亿但仍引发远超其规模的关注。
③P3: 后果——收藏家远离市场，当代艺术销售锐减 2/3 至 90%，两大拍卖行被迫支付近 2 亿美元保证金。
④P4: 当前状况——自 1989 年以来最严重衰退，价格平均跌 40%，但佳士得 CEO 认为已见底。
⑤P5: 此次衰退不同之处——仍有买家但缺好作品；死亡/债务/离婚仍输送作品，但非卖家选择观望。

📝 题目答案速查 ▼ 展开

21In the first paragraph, Damien Hirst's sale was referred to as "a last victory" because ______.
A. the art market had witnessed a succession of victories B. the auctioneer finally got the two pieces at the highest bids C. Beautiful Inside My Head Forever won over all masterpieces D. it was successfully made just before the world financial crisis ✓
参考答案：D

🔍 定位：P1 S03-S04 — "It was a last victory. As the auctioneer called out bids, ... Lehman Brothers filed for bankruptcy."

✅ 推理：作者先说这是"最后的胜利"，紧接着说就在拍卖进行时雷曼兄弟申请破产（标志金融危机爆发）→ 这次成功拍卖正好发生在金融危机之前 → 此后市场崩溃。

❌ 选项分析：
A. witnessed a succession of victories（见证了一系列胜利）— 文中没说，victories 也无据可循。
B. the auctioneer finally got the two pieces（拍卖师最终拿到了那两件拍品）— S02 说 all but two pieces sold（除两件外全卖了），这两件是没卖出去的，不是拍卖师拿到。
C. won over all masterpieces（胜过了所有杰作）— 夸大其词，文中只说创了纪录。
D. was made just before the world financial crisis（成功地在世界金融危机前夕做成）— ✅ 对应 S04 雷曼破产的时间点。

22By saying "spending of any sort became deeply unfashionable" (Line 1~2, Para. 3), the author suggests that ______.
A. collectors were no longer actively involved in art-market auctions ✓ B. people stopped every kind of spending and stayed away from galleries C. art collection as a fashion had lost its appeal to a great extent D. works of art in general had gone out of fashion so they were not worth buying
参考答案：A

🔍 定位：P3 S09-S10 — "spending of any sort became deeply unfashionable. In the art world that meant collectors stayed away from galleries and salerooms."

✅ 推理：第二句 directly 解释了第一句的意思："unfashionable" 在艺术界的具体表现是收藏家远离画廊和拍卖厅 → 不再积极参与拍卖。

❌ 选项分析：
A. collectors were no longer actively involved — ✅ 对应 stayed away from galleries and salerooms。
B. people stopped every kind of spending — ❌ 程度过强！文中只是说"any sort of spending"变得不流行，但不是完全停止（stopped）。
C. art collection as a fashion had lost its appeal — unfashionable 虽然字面是"不时尚"，但文中不是指艺术收藏过时了，而是指花钱购买艺术品这个行为不受欢迎了。
D. works of art ... not worth buying — not worth buying（不值得买）文中未说，是过度引申。

23Which of the following statements is NOT true?
A. Sales of contemporary art fell dramatically from 2007 to 2008. B. The art market surpassed many other industries in momentum. ✓ C. The art market generally went downward in various ways. D. Some art dealers were awaiting better chances to come.
参考答案：B（NOT true）

🔍 定位：逐项回文定位。

✅ 推理：A 对应 P3 S11 "Sales of contemporary art fell by two-thirds" → TRUE。C 全文多处提到市场下行 → TRUE。D 对应 S19 "anyone who does not have to sell is keeping away, waiting for confidence to return" → TRUE。B 对应 S08 "the market generates interest far beyond its size ... in a way matched by few other industries" — 注意：原文说的是 interest/关注度远超规模，不是 momentum/增长动力！且"surpassed"（超越）与 "matched by few"（很少有能匹敌的）方向一致但对象错了。

❌ B 为什么错（NOT true）：原文说艺术市场引发的关注度很少有其他行业能匹敌（interest far beyond its size），而不是说它在增长动力上超越了其他行业。偷换概念（interest → momentum）。

24The three Ds mentioned in the last paragraph are ______.
A. auction houses' favorites B. contemporary trends C. factors promoting artwork circulation ✓ D. styles representing Impressionists
参考答案：C

🔍 定位：P5 S18 — "The three Ds—death, debt and divorce—still deliver works of art to the market."

✅ 推理：three Ds 本身就是 death, debt, divorce，它们的作用是 deliver works of art to the market（将艺术品输送到市场）→ 即促进艺术品流通的因素（factors promoting artwork circulation）。

❌ 选项分析：A. auction houses' favorites（拍卖行的最爱）— 文中没说是 auction houses 的 favorites。B. contemporary trends（当代潮流）— 死亡/债务/离婚不是"潮流"。D. styles representing Impressionists（代表印象派的风格）— 完全无关。

25The most appropriate title for this text could be ______.
A. Fluctuation of Art Prices B. Up-to-date Art Auctions C. Art Market in Decline ✓ D. Shifted Interest in Arts
参考答案：C

🔍 定位：全文主旨。文章开篇讲牛市终结（bull run ended），中间讲 sales fell by two-thirds、downturn、slump，结尾讲买家观望。

✅ 推理：全文都在描述艺术市场的衰退（decline/downturn/slump）→ C 最贴切。

❌ 选项分析：
A. Fluctuation of Art Prices（艺术价格的波动）— 价格波动只是文中一小部分，非主旨。
B. Up-to-date Art Auctions（最新的艺术拍卖）— 只覆盖第一段，太窄。
C. Art Market in Decline（衰退中的艺术市场）— ✅ 概括全文。
D. Shifted Interest in Arts（对艺术兴趣的转移）— 文中没提兴趣转移，说的是经济衰退导致消费减少。
