男女沟通差异与婚姻危机2010 考研英语（二）· Text 2

This episode crystallizes the irony — men talk more in public, less at home

难度 考研级 词汇·语法·结构 主题 · 社会关系 长难句 5
Paragraph 1 — 一场聚会中的对话：丈夫在外侃侃而谈，在家沉默寡言
1
I was addressing a small gathering in a suburban Virginia living room—a women's group that had invited men to join them.
我在弗吉尼亚郊区一间客厅里对一个小型聚会发表讲话——这是一个邀请了男性参加的妇女团体。
🔍 语法解析
主干：I was addressing a small gathering（主+谓+宾），过去进行时，描述故事背景。
同位语：破折号后的 a women's group that had invited men to join them 是 gathering 的同位语，具体说明这是什么性质的聚会。
词义：address 对……讲话（熟词生义，不是"地址"）；suburban 郊区的。
2
Throughout the evening, one man had been particularly talkative, frequently offering ideas and anecdotes, while his wife sat silently beside him on the couch.
整晚，有一个男人特别健谈，频频献计献策、讲述趣闻轶事，而他的妻子则静静地坐在他身旁的沙发上。
🔍 语法解析
主干：one man had been particularly talkative（主+系+表），过去完成进行时，强调持续整个晚上的状态。
伴随状语：frequently offering ideas and anecdotes → 现在分词短语，补充说明 talkative 的具体表现。
while 从句：while his wife sat silently beside him on the couch → 对比（while = 而/却），形成鲜明对照——丈夫健谈 vs 妻子沉默。
词义：talkative 健谈的；anecdote 趣闻轶事。
3
Toward the end of the evening, I commented that women frequently complain that their husbands don't talk to them.
临近晚会结束时，我评论说女性经常抱怨丈夫不跟她们说话。
🔍 语法解析
主干：I commented that ...（主+谓+宾语从句）。
嵌套宾语从句：that women frequently complain that their husbands don't talk to them — 第一层 that 作 commented 的宾语；第二层 that 作 complain 的宾语。
词义：comment 评论 / 说道。
4
This man quickly nodded in agreement.
这个男人赶紧点头表示同意。
🔍 语法解析
主干：This man nodded（主+谓）。in agreement 作方式状语。
词义：nodded in agreement 点头同意。
5
He gestured toward his wife and said, "She's the talker in our family."
他朝妻子做了个手势说："她才是我们家那个爱说话的人。"
🔍 语法解析
并列谓语：gestured and said（两个动词并列），说明他先做手势后说话。
直接引语：said 后接直接引语，引用原话。
6
The room burst into laughter; the man looked puzzled and hurt.
满屋子哄堂大笑；这个男人看上去既困惑又受伤。
🔍 语法解析
并列句：分号连接两个独立分句，形成对比——别人觉得好笑（burst into laughter），他却感到不解和受伤（puzzled and hurt）。
词义：burst into 突然……起来；puzzled 困惑的。
7
"It's true," he explained.
"这是真的，"他解释道。
🔍 语法解析
引述倒装：he explained 置于引语之后，是常见的"引述倒装"结构。
8
"When I come home from work, I have nothing to say. If she didn't keep the conversation going, we'd spend the whole evening in silence."
"我下班回家没什么可说的。要不是她一直说话，我们整晚都会在沉默中度过。"
🔍 长难句拆解
第一句：When I come home（时间状语从句）+ I have nothing to say（主句），陈述事实。
第二句：If she didn't keep the conversation going（虚拟条件句，与现在事实相反），we'd spend the whole evening in silence（虚拟主句）。
💡 虚拟语气：If + 过去时(didn't keep) → would + 动词原形(spend)。表示"如果她不……（但实际上她在做），我们就会……"。这是与现在事实相反的虚拟条件句。
词义：keep sth. going 使……持续下去。
Paragraph 2 — 反讽：美国男性在外话多，在家话少
9
This episode crystallizes the irony that although American men tend to talk more than women in public situations, they often talk less at home.
这个小插曲揭示了一个反讽的现象：尽管美国男性在公共场合比女性话多，在家却往往话更少。
🔍 长难句拆解
主干：This episode crystallizes the irony（主+谓+宾）。
同位语从句：that although American men tend to talk more than women in public situations, they often talk less at home → 解释 irony（反讽）的具体内容。从句内含 although 引导的让步状语从句。
💡 句式逻辑：although ...（公众场合话多）vs ...（在家话少），形成对比。
词义：crystallize 使具体化 / 使明确（原义"结晶"，引申义"使变得清晰"）；irony 反讽 / 具有讽刺意味的事。
10
And this pattern is wreaking havoc with marriage.
这一模式正在对婚姻造成严重破坏。
🔍 语法解析
主干：this pattern is wreaking havoc with marriage（主+谓+宾）。
时态：现在进行时 is wreaking，强调这个模式正在持续造成破坏。
词义：wreak havoc 造成严重破坏（wreak = 造成，havoc = 浩劫/大破坏），是 Q27 的考点词。
Paragraph 3 — 社会学研究：沟通不畅是离婚的主因
11
The pattern was observed by political scientist Andrew Hacker in the late 1970s.
政治学家 Andrew Hacker 早在 20 世纪 70 年代末就观察到了这一模式。
🔍 语法解析
被动语态：The pattern was observed by ...，强调"这一模式被观察到"，而非"谁观察到了"。
12
Sociologist Catherine Kohler Riessman reports in her new book Divorce Talk that most of the women she interviewed—but only a few of the men—gave lack of communication as the reason for their divorces.
社会学家 Catherine Kohler Riessman 在其新书《离婚对话》中报告说，她采访的大多数女性——但只有少数男性——将缺乏沟通列为离婚的原因。
🔍 长难句拆解
主干：Sociologist Catherine Kohler Riessman reports ... that ...（主+谓+宾语从句）。
宾语从句：most of the women ... gave lack of communication as the reason for their divorces。
  ① she interviewed → 省略关系代词的定语从句修饰 women
  ② but only a few of the men → 破折号插入语，与 most of the women 形成对比
词义：lack of communication 缺乏沟通；give ... as the reason 将……作为理由。
13
Given the current divorce rate of nearly 50 percent, that amounts to millions of cases in the United States every year—a virtual epidemic of failed conversation.
考虑到目前近 50% 的离婚率，这意味着美国每年有数百万例——一场名副其实的沟通失败流行病。
🔍 长难句拆解
主干：that amounts to millions of cases（主+谓+宾）。
条件状语：Given the current divorce rate of nearly 50 percent — Given 在此为介词"考虑到/鉴于"，不是"给予"。
同位语：a virtual epidemic of failed conversation → 补充说明 millions of cases，将其比喻为"流行病"。
词义：amount to 总计 / 相当于；epidemic 流行病（此处为暗喻）；virtual 实际上 / 几乎。
Paragraph 4 — 妻子的核心诉求：丈夫成为对话伙伴
14
In my own research, complaints from women about their husbands most often focused not on tangible inequities such as having given up the chance for a career to accompany a husband to his, or doing far more than their share of daily life-support work like cleaning, cooking and social arrangements.
在我自己的研究中，女性对丈夫的抱怨大多并非集中于那些有形的、不平等的事情上——比如放弃自己的职业机会去配合丈夫的事业，或者在打扫、烹饪和社交安排等日常支撑性工作中承担远超自己份额的部分。
🔍 长难句拆解
主干：complaints ... focused not on ...（主+谓+宾）。主干很短，但宾语部分非常长。
not on 后的列举：
  ① having given up the chance for a career to accompany a husband to his → 放弃事业机会去配合丈夫
  ② doing far more than their share of daily life-support work → 承担远超份额的家务
such as 引出两个并列的动名词短语作举例。
💡 Q28 考点：此句清楚说明妻子的抱怨不是关于 tangible inequities（有形的不平等），而是关于 communication（沟通），为后文做铺垫。
词义：tangible 有形的 / 具体的；inequity 不公平（in- 否定 + equity 公平）；accompany 陪伴。
15
Instead they focused on communication: "He doesn't listen to me." "He doesn't talk to me."
相反，她们关注的是沟通："他不听我说话。""他不跟我说话。"
🔍 语法解析
转折：Instead 与上一句的 not on tangible inequities 形成对比——"不是……而是……"。
直接引语：冒号后引用女性实际说的话，增加真实感。
16
I found, as Hacker observed years before, that most wives want their husbands to be, first and foremost, conversational partners, but few husbands share this expectation of their wives.
正如 Hacker 多年前观察到的那样，我发现大多数妻子都希望自己的丈夫首先是一个对话伙伴，但很少有丈夫对妻子抱有同样的期望。
🔍 长难句拆解
主干：I found ... that ...（主+谓+宾语从句）。
插入语：as Hacker observed years before（正如 Hacker 多年前观察到的那样）→ 插入在 found 和 that 之间，交代研究发现的依据。
宾语从句：most wives want their husbands to be ... conversational partners, but few husbands share this expectation of their wives → but 连接两个并列分句，形成对比——妻子希望丈夫是"对话伙伴"，但丈夫不这么期望妻子。
词义：first and foremost 首先（写作高频插入语）；conversational partner 对话伙伴。
Paragraph 5 — 一个经典的漫画画面
17
In short, the image that best represents the current crisis is the stereotypical cartoon scene of a man sitting at the breakfast table with a newspaper held up in front of his face, while a woman glares at the back of it, wanting to talk.
简而言之，最能代表当前危机的一幕是那个刻板印象中的漫画场景：一个男人坐在早餐桌前，举着一张报纸挡在面前，而一个女人瞪着报纸背面，想要说话。
🔍 长难句拆解
主干：the image ... is the stereotypical cartoon scene（主+系+表）。
定语从句：that best represents the current crisis → 修饰 image。
scene 的修饰：of a man sitting ... while a woman glaring ... → 介词短语作后置定语，详细描述这个场景。
  ① a man sitting at the breakfast table with a newspaper held up in front of his face → with + n. + 过去分词(hheld up)，独立主格结构
  ② while a woman glares at the back of it, wanting to talk → while 对比 + 现在分词 wanting 作伴随状语
💡 修辞：用一个生动的画面结束全文，将抽象的"沟通危机"具象化。这个画面本身就是 Q30 的线索——文章接下来很可能详细描述这幅漫画。
词义：stereotypical 刻板印象的；glare at 怒视。

📋 全文脉络 ▲ 收起

① P1: 开场故事——聚会上丈夫在外健谈，在家沉默，引发哄笑。
② P2: 点题——男性公众场合话多、在家话少，这一模式正在破坏婚姻。
③ P3: 研究证据——Hacker 早期观察 + Riessman 新书数据：沟通缺失是离婚主因。

📝 题目答案速查 ▼ 展开

26. What is most wives' main expectation of their husbands?
[A] Talking to them. [B] Trusting them. [C] Supporting their careers. [D] Sharing housework.
参考答案：A

✅ 正确答案：A（Talking to them 和她们说话）

定位：P4 · S16 — "most wives want their husbands to be, first and foremost, conversational partners"

推理：conversational partners（对话伙伴）= talking to them。且前文 S15 直接引用了妻子们的话："He doesn't listen to me. He doesn't talk to me."进一步证实妻子最核心的期望是丈夫和她们交谈。

❌ B 错误：Trusting them（信任她们）——文中未提及信任问题。

❌ C 错误：Supporting their careers（支持她们的事业）——S14 提到"giving up the chance for a career to accompany a husband to his"（放弃自己的事业去配合丈夫），但作者说的是这不是妻子抱怨的重点。

❌ D 错误：Sharing housework（分担家务）——同上，S14 说"doing far more than their share of daily life-support work like cleaning, cooking"也不是妻子抱怨的重点。妻子最在意的是沟通。

27. Judging from the context, the phrase "wreaking havoc" (Line 2, Para. 2) most probably means _______.
[A] generating motivation [B] exerting influence [C] causing damage [D] creating pressure
参考答案：C

✅ 正确答案：C（causing damage 造成破坏）

定位：P2 · S10 — "this pattern is wreaking havoc with marriage" — 紧接着 P3 就用离婚率数据（nearly 50 percent, millions of cases, epidemic of failed conversation）来展开说明。

推理：下文提到离婚率高达 50%、每年数百万例离婚、沟通失败的"epidemic"。这些词都具有强烈的负面含义，说明 wrecking havoc 一定是一个负面、破坏性的意思。havoc = 大破坏/浩劫，wreak = 造成。causing damage = 造成破坏，最接近。

❌ A 错误：generating motivation（激发动力）——正面意思，与上下文负面语境不符。

❌ B 错误：exerting influence（施加影响）——中性词，文中语境是负面的。

❌ D 错误：creating pressure（制造压力）——虽然有点负面，但 pressure 的程度远不及 havoc（浩劫/大破坏），且与离婚率的"破坏性"不匹配。

28. All of the following are true EXCEPT _______.
[A] men tend to talk more in public than women [B] nearly 50 percent of recent divorces are caused by failed conversation [C] women attach much importance to communication between couples [D] a female tends to be more talkative at home than her spouse
参考答案：B（NOT true）

✅ 正确答案：B（nearly 50 percent of recent divorces are caused by failed conversation）——注意本题选 NOT true

定位：逐项回文——A→P2 S09，B→P3 S13，C→P4 S15-16，D→P1 S02。

A 正确：P2 S09 "American men tend to talk more than women in public situations" → ✅。

❌ B 错误（本题答案）：P3 S13 "Given the current divorce rate of nearly 50 percent, that amounts to millions of cases" — 注意！50% 是离婚率（divorce rate），不是"由沟通失败导致的离婚比例"。原文说"考虑到近 50% 的离婚率……那意味着数百万例"，但没有说这数百万例全部都是沟通失败导致的。选项 B 偷换了概念——把 divorce rate 变成了 caused by failed conversation 的比例。这是考研阅读中典型的"偷换概念"干扰手法。

C 正确：P4 S15-16 全段都在讲妻子重视沟通 → ✅。

D 正确：P1 S02 "his wife sat silently beside him" + S05 "She's the talker in our family" → 妻子在家更健谈 → ✅。

29. Which of the following can best summarize the main idea of this text?
[A] The moral decaying deserves more research by sociologists. [B] Marriage break-up stems from sex inequalities. [C] Husband and wife have different expectations from their marriage. [D] Conversational patterns between man and wife are different.
参考答案：D

✅ 正确答案：D（Conversational patterns between man and wife are different 夫妻之间的对话模式不同）

定位：全文主旨题。文章的核心线索：P1 丈夫在外健谈在家沉默 → P2 点题（talk more in public, talk less at home）→ P3-P4 沟通缺失导致离婚 → P5 漫画场景收尾。

推理：全文都围绕"男性和女性在沟通模式上的差异"展开——外在公共场所 vs 在家、健谈 vs 沉默、想说话 vs 不想说话。D 选项最全面准确地概括了这一主旨。

❌ A 错误：The moral decaying deserves more research（道德衰退需要更多研究）——文章讲的是沟通模式差异，不是"道德衰退"。

❌ B 错误：Marriage break-up stems from sex inequalities（婚姻破裂源于性别不平等）——S14 明确说妻子抱怨的重点不是 tangible inequities（有形的不平等），而是沟通问题。

❌ C 错误：Husband and wife have different expectations from their marriage（夫妻对婚姻有不同期望）——虽然 S16 提到 different expectations，但具体来说是"沟通"层面的期望不同（conversational partners），C 选项过于宽泛。

30. In the following part immediately after this text, the author will most probably focus on _______.
[A] a vivid account of the new book Divorce Talk [B] a detailed description of the stereotypical cartoon [C] other possible reasons for a high divorce rate in the U.S. [D] a brief introduction to the political scientist Andrew Hacker
参考答案：B

✅ 正确答案：B（a detailed description of the stereotypical cartoon 对那幅刻板漫画的详细描述）

定位：P5 · S17 — 文章以一幅漫画画面收尾："the stereotypical cartoon scene of a man sitting at the breakfast table with a newspaper held up in front of his face, while a woman glares at the back of it, wanting to talk"

推理："In short"和"the image that best represents the current crisis"——作者用"最能代表"和"简而言之"表明这是全文的总结升华。同时，文章在最后一句话抛出了一个生动的画面，但还没有展开讨论。按写作逻辑，下一段很可能围绕这幅漫画进行详细描述。C 选项的答案我不确定，但这更符合续写逻辑。

等一下，我重新考虑一下。这篇文章主要讲男女沟通差异对婚姻的影响，结尾用漫画收尾。那接下来最可能做什么？应该是进一步讨论这个现象，而不是跳回到前面的内容。B 选项（detailed description of the stereotypical cartoon）是最自然的延续——既然在结尾提到了这幅漫画，下一段很可能展开描述它。

❌ A 错误：《Divorce Talk》是 P3 提到的，已经介绍过了，不太可能回头再讲。

❌ C 错误：other possible reasons for a high divorce rate——文章已经论证了沟通问题这个原因，不太可能突然转向其他原因。

❌ D 错误：Andrew Hacker 是 P3 顺带提及的人物，不太可能专门介绍他。
