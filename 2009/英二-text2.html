2009 年考研英语（二）Text 2 精读

DNA Testing · 亲子鉴定与基因测试的商业化争议
考研英语（二） 六级~考研级 Text 2 / 共 4 篇 17 句 + 5 道题
📊 难度推断：六级~考研级（依据：考研词表外词汇占比约 18%，非文章来源限制）
S01 It is a wise father that knows his own child, but today a man can boost his paternal (fatherly) wisdom—or at least confirm that he's the kid's dad.
🌐 译文
再聪明的父亲也未必认得自己的孩子，但如今一个男人可以提升自己身为父亲的智慧——或者至少能确认自己是不是孩子的爹。
语法分析
主干a man can boost his paternal wisdom — or at least confirm that he's the kid's dad
从句It is a wise father that knows his own child — 谚语式让步（反讽用法）：再聪明的父亲也未必认得自己的孩子
修饰or at least confirm that he's the kid's dad — 与前半句"boost"并列，由 or 连接，补充最低保证
语法标签谚语式让步句（It is...that...）、破折号插入语、or 并列、that 宾语从句
词汇paternal adj. 父亲的 / boost v. 提升 / confirm v. 确认
S02 All he needs to do is shell out $30 for a paternity testing kit (PTK) at his local drugstore—and another $120 to get the results.
🌐 译文
他只需到本地药店花 30 美元买一套亲子鉴定试剂盒（PTK），再花 120 美元拿到结果就行。
语法分析
主干All (that) he needs to do is shell out $30 for a PTK at his local drugstore — and (shell out) another $120 to get the results
结构定语从句（all that 省略）、and 省略结构（避免重复 shell out）、破折号连接并列
词汇shell out v. 支付 / paternity n. 父亲身份 / kit n. 试剂盒 / drugstore n. 药店
熟词生义shell 通常义"贝壳/炮弹"，生义 v. 支付（shell out = 掏钱）
S03 More than 60,000 people have purchased the PTKs since they first become available without prescriptions last year, according to Doug Fogg, chief operating officer of Identigene, which makes the over-the-counter kits.
🌐 译文
据 Identigene（生产这套非处方试剂盒的厂家）首席运营官 Doug Fogg 称，自去年该试剂盒首次无需处方即可购买以来，已有逾 6 万人购买。
语法分析
主干More than 60,000 people have purchased the PTKs since they first became available without prescriptions last year
结构since 时间状语从句、according to 插入语、which 非限定性定语从句（修饰 Identigene）
词汇purchase v. 购买（正式） / available adj. 可获得的 / prescription n. 处方 / chief operating officer 首席运营官（COO）/ over-the-counter adj. 非处方的（OTC）
S04 More than two dozen companies sell DNA tests directly to the public, ranging in price from a few hundred dollars to more than $2,500.
🌐 译文
有 20 多家公司直接向公众销售 DNA 检测服务，价格从几百美元到 2500 多美元不等。
语法分析
主干More than two dozen companies sell DNA tests directly to the public
修饰现在分词 ranging in price from... to... 作伴随状语，说明价格范围
词汇a dozen n. 一打、12 个 / range v. 在…范围内变动 / directly adv. 直接地
S05 Among the most popular: paternity and kinship testing, which adopted children can use to find their biological relatives and families can use to track down kids put up for adoption.
🌐 译文
最受欢迎的检测包括：亲子鉴定与亲缘鉴定——前者供被收养的孩子寻找亲生亲属，后者供家庭追踪送养的孩子。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干paternity and kinship testing
which...adopted children can use [it] to find their biological relatives
and families can use [it] to track down kids (that are) put up for adoption
结构which 非限定性定语从句、并列目的状语（can use to... and can use to...）、put up 被动式
词汇kinship n. 血缘关系 / biological adj. 亲生的 / relative n. 亲属 / track down 追查到 / put up for adoption 送养
S06 DNA testing is also the latest rage among passionate genealogists—and supports businesses that offer to search for a family's geographic roots.
🌐 译文
DNA 检测在狂热的家谱爱好者中也是当下最流行的活动——同时也支撑了一些提供家族地理溯源服务的公司。
语法分析
主干DNA testing is the latest rage among passionate genealogists — and supports businesses that offer to search for a family's geographic roots
结构and 并列谓语、that 定语从句（修饰 businesses）
词汇rage n. 风行 / passionate adj. 狂热的 / genealogist n. 谱系学家 / geographic adj. 地理的 / roots n. 祖籍
熟词生义rage 通常义"愤怒"，生义 n. 风行、时尚（the latest rage）
S07 Most tests require collecting cells by swabbing saliva in the mouth and sending it to the company for testing.
🌐 译文
大多数检测要求用棉签擦拭口腔内壁采集唾液细胞，然后将其送至公司进行检测。
语法分析
主干Most tests require collecting cells by A and sending it to the company for testing
结构require + 动名词（collecting / sending）、by + doing 方式状语、and 并列动名词
词汇swab v. 擦拭取样 / saliva n. 唾液
S08 All tests require a potential candidate with whom to compare DNA.
🌐 译文
所有检测都需要一个潜在的比对对象，以便与其进行 DNA 比对。
语法分析
主干All tests require a potential candidate
后置定语with whom to compare DNA = to compare DNA with whom（"介词 + 关系代词 + 不定式"复合结构）
语法点睛whom 指代 candidate，提前到 with 之后
词汇candidate n. 候选对象、样本
S09 But some observers are skeptical.
🌐 译文
但一些观察家持怀疑态度。
语法分析
主干some observers are skeptical
结构but 转折、形容词作表语
词汇skeptical adj. 持怀疑态度的（= sceptical）
S10 "There's a kind of false precision being hawked by people claiming they are doing ancestry testing," says Troy Duster, a New York University sociologist.
🌐 译文
"那些声称在做祖源检测的人正在兜售一种虚假的精确性，"纽约大学社会学家 Troy Duster 说。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干"There's a kind of false precision being hawked by people claiming they are doing ancestry testing," says Troy Duster
结构倒装句（There be）、被动语态（being hawked）、现在分词 claiming 作后置定语、says 倒装（直接引语在前）
词汇false precision n. 虚假的精确性 / hawk v. 兜售 / ancestry n. 祖籍 / sociologist n. 社会学家
熟词生义hawk 通常义"n. 鹰"，生义 v. 兜售、叫卖
S11 He notes that each individual has many ancestors—numbering in the hundreds just a few centuries back.
🌐 译文
他指出，每个人都有许多祖先——仅追溯到几百年前，祖先数量就达数百人。
语法分析
主干He notes that each individual has many ancestors
结构that 宾语从句、破折号 + 现在分词 numbering in the hundreds 作伴随状语
词汇individual n. 个人 / ancestor n. 祖先 / number v. 数量达
S12 Yet most ancestry testing only considers a single lineage, either the Y chromosome inherited through men in a father's line or mitochondrial DNA, which is passed down only from mothers.
🌐 译文
然而大多数祖源检测只考察单一血统，要么是父系传承的 Y 染色体，要么是仅从母亲传下来的线粒体 DNA。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干most ancestry testing only considers a single lineage, either A or B
Athe Y chromosome (which is) inherited through men in a father's line → 过去分词作后置定语
Bmitochondrial DNA, which is passed down only from mothers → 非限定性定语从句
结构either...or... 并列、过去分词 inherited、which 非限定性定语从句
词汇lineage n. 血统 / chromosome n. 染色体 / inherit v. 继承 / mitochondrial adj. 线粒体的 / pass down 传下来
S13 This DNA can reveal genetic information about only one or two ancestors, even though, for example, just three generations back people also have six other great-grandparents or, four generations back, 14 other great-great-grandparents.
🌐 译文
这种 DNA 只能揭示一两位祖先的基因信息，例如：仅往上追溯三代，人们就有另外 6 位曾祖父母；再往上追溯四代，就还有 14 位高祖父母。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干This DNA can reveal genetic information about only one or two ancestors
从句even though, for example, just three generations back people also have six other great-grandparents or, four generations back, 14 other great-great-grandparents — 让步状语从句
结构even though 让步、for example 插入语、or 并列、back adv. 以前（熟词生义）
数学彩蛋N 代祖先数 = 2^(N+1) − 2：三代 = 14（其中"另外的"曾祖父母 = 8 − 2 = 6）
词汇generation n. 一代、世代 / great-grandparent n. 曾祖父母
S14 Critics also argue that commercial genetic testing is only as good as the reference collections to which a sample is compared.
🌐 译文
批评者还指出，商业基因检测的好坏取决于样本对比所参照的数据库。
语法分析
主干Critics also argue that...
宾从commercial genetic testing is only as good as the reference collections to which a sample is compared
结构that 宾语从句、as...as... 比较结构、to which 介词提前的定语从句
句型积累X is only as good as Y = X 的好坏取决于 Y。例：A teacher is only as good as the students she teaches.
词汇critic n. 批评者 / commercial adj. 商业的 / genetic adj. 基因的 / reference n. 参考 / collection n. 收集、样本库
S15 Databases used by some companies don't rely on data collected systematically but rather lump together information from different research projects.
🌐 译文
一些公司使用的数据库不依赖于系统化采集的数据，而是把不同研究项目的信息混在一起。
语法分析
主干Databases (which are) used by some companies don't rely on data (which are) collected systematically but rather lump together information from different research projects
结构过去分词 used / collected 作后置定语、not...but rather... 转折、from 不同来源
短语lump together = 不加区分地混在一起（隐含贬义）
词汇systematically adv. 系统地 / research project n. 研究项目
S16 This means that a DNA database may have a lot of data from some regions and not others, so a person's test results may differ depending on the company that processes the results.
🌐 译文
这意味着一个 DNA 数据库可能有某个地区的大量数据，而其他地区的数据则很少，因此同一个人在不同公司做检测，结果可能不同。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干This means that A, so B
Aa DNA database may have a lot of data from some regions and not others（部分否定）
Ba person's test results may differ depending on the company that processes the results
结构that 宾语从句、so 结果状语从句、depending on 现在分词作状语、that 定语从句
词汇database n. 数据库 / differ v. 不同
S17 In addition, the computer programs (that) a company uses to estimate relationships may be patented and not subject to peer review or outside evaluation.
🌐 译文
此外，公司用来估算亲缘关系的计算机程序可能申请了专利，从而不接受同行评审或外部评估。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干the computer programs (that) a company uses to estimate relationships may be patented and (may be) not subject to peer review or outside evaluation
结构定语从句（that 省略）、and 省略结构（避免重复 may be）、not subject to... 形容词短语
词汇in addition 此外 / estimate v. 估计 / patent v. 申请专利 / peer review 同行评审 / evaluation n. 评估 / be subject to 受…支配、接受…

全文脉络收起

① 引入话题（S01-02）：聪明的父亲未必认得自己的孩子，但现在有 PTK 可以确认。
② 市场背景（S03-04）：6 万人购买、20+ 家公司销售，价格从几百到 2500+ 美元。
③ 主要用途（S05-06）：亲子鉴定 / 亲缘鉴定 / 家族地理溯源。
④ 检测流程（S07-08）：擦拭口腔 + 比对 DNA。
⑤ 质疑与缺陷（S09-13）：观察家指出 DNA 只能揭示 1-2 位祖先，无法反映数百位祖先的完整信息。
⑥ 更多批评（S14-17）：数据库不系统化、程序不公开受评审，结果因公司而异。
作者态度：态度中立偏负——既呈现 DNA 检测的流行与便利，也着重揭示其局限与商业问题；结尾 S17 暗示对行业缺乏监管的担忧。

26. In Paragraphs 1 and 2, the text shows PTK's ___.
[A] easy availability ✓ [B] flexibility in pricing [C] successful promotion [D] popularity with households
你的答案：A ✓ 正确

🔍 定位：P1 "shell out $30 for a paternity testing kit at his local drugstore" + P2 "available without prescriptions"、"More than two dozen companies sell DNA tests directly to the public"

✅ 正确答案推理：P1 显示 PTK 在普通药店就能买到（仅 30 美元），P2 进一步说明无需处方即可购买、有 20 多家公司直接销售——所有证据都指向"容易获得"（easy availability）。

❌ B 选项：P2 提到 "ranging in price from a few hundred dollars to more than $2,500"，但这只说明 DNA 检测整个市场的价格区间，并非同一家 PTK 产品的价格弹性——P1 明确 PTK 固定 30 美元 + 120 美元出结果。

❌ C 选项："successful promotion"（成功促销）文中有销售数据但未涉及任何"促销手段"（如打折、广告），属于无中生有。

❌ D 选项："popularity with households"（家庭普及度）文中未提及家庭层面的采用率数据，60,000+ 的购买者 ≠ 家庭普及度。

📌 用户答案点评：选 A 正确 ✓ 判断准确。

27. PTK is used to ___.
[A] locate one's birth place [B] promote genetic research [C] identify parent-child kinship ✓ [D] choose children for adoption
你的答案：C ✓ 正确

🔍 定位：P3 "Among the most popular: paternity and kinship testing"

✅ 正确答案推理：P3 直接点出 PTK 最主要用途是"paternity and kinship testing"（亲子和亲缘鉴定），即"识别父母与子女的血缘关系"。S02 中的 "confirm that he's the kid's dad" 也是直接证据。

❌ A 选项：地理溯源（geographic roots）是另一项服务（genealogy），并非 PTK 本身的用途，且与"出生地"是不同概念。

❌ B 选项：PTK 是商业产品（commercial），文中未涉基因研究推动。

❌ D 选项：收养是 P3 提到的应用场景之一，但 PTK 用来追踪已被收养的孩子找亲生父母，而非"挑选"被收养的孩子。

📌 用户答案点评：选 C 正确 ✓ 一眼锁定。

28. Skeptical observers believe that ancestry testing fails to ___.
[A] trace distant ancestors [B] rebuild reliable bloodlines [C] fully use genetic information [D] achieve the claimed accuracy ✓
你的答案：D ✓ 正确

🔍 定位：P5 "Some critics argue that commercial genetic testing is 'needlessly obscure'... and the tests themselves are not as accurate as they claim... Others... point to the 'false precision'..." + P6 "Databases used by some companies don't rely on data collected systematically..."

✅ 正确答案推理：题干主语是 "skeptical observers"（怀疑的观察者 = 原文的 critics / skeptics），他们认为商业基因检测 无法达到其声称的精确度（fail to achieve the claimed accuracy）。原文 P5 直接点名 "false precision"（虚假的精确性）、测试 "not as accurate as they claim"（没它们声称的那么准），以及 P6 数据库不系统化导致结果因公司而异——都指向"声称的精度不可靠"，D 精准对应。

❌ A 选项：trace distant ancestors——文章反而说线粒体 DNA / Y 染色体能追溯母系单一血统到 generations back（曾祖、高祖），即检测 确实能 trace 一些 distant ancestors；怀疑者批评的是"精度/准确度"，不是"完全不能追溯"，故 A 不是 skeptics 的核心论点。

❌ B 选项：rebuild reliable bloodlines——原文未提 "rebuild" 或 "reliable"，属无中生有。

❌ C 选项：fully use genetic information——虽有"只揭示 1-2 位祖先信息"的覆盖窄问题，但题干问的是 skeptics 认为检测 "fails to ___"，原文直接点名的是 "claimed accuracy / false precision"（精度层面），C 偏"范围层面"，不如 D 精准贴合 skeptics 原话。
