教育与经济增长的真相

📱 v2 兼容修复版（2026-07-20）· The relationship between formal education and economic growth in poor countries — 逐句精读 · 词汇 · 语法 · 命题分析

📊 难度推断：考研级。本文含经济学/教育学学术词汇（misunderstood, conventional view, productivity, counterpart 等），长难句密集，含倒装、同位语从句、同位结构等考研核心语法点。
Paragraph 1 — 传统观点认为教育是穷国发展的关键——但作者说：错
S01 The relationship between formal education and economic growth in poor countries is widely misunderstood by economists and politicians alike.
🌐 译文
正规教育与穷国经济增长之间的关系被经济学家和政治家们广泛误解了。
语法分析
主干The relationship ... is widely misunderstood by economists and politicians alike（被动语态）
修饰between formal education and economic growth in poor countries → 介词短语作后置定语修饰 relationship
词义misunderstood 被误解的（mis-错误 + understood）· alike adv. 同样地（both...and...）
S02 Progress in both areas is undoubtedly necessary for the social, political and intellectual development of these and all other societies;
🌐 译文
这两个领域（教育与经济）的进步对这些国家及所有其他社会的社会、政治和智力发展无疑都是必要的。
语法分析
主干Progress ... is undoubtedly necessary ...（主系表）
修饰in both areas → 介词短语后置定语修饰 Progress；for ... development → 范围状语；of these and all other societies → 后置定语修饰 development
词义undoubtedly 毫无疑问地 · intellectual 智力的
S03 However, the conventional view that education should be one of the very highest priorities for promoting rapid economic development in poor countries is wrong.
🌐 译文
然而，那种认为教育应成为促进穷国经济快速发展的最优先事项之一的传统观点是错误的。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干the conventional view ... is wrong（主系表）
修饰that education ... in poor countries → 同位语从句（解释 view 的内容，that 在从句中不作成分）
语法同位语从句是考研高频考点！that 引导同位语从句时只起连接作用，不充当成分；而定语从句中 that 要充当主/宾语。
词义conventional 传统的 · priority 优先事项 · promote 促进
S04 We are fortunate that it is, because building new educational systems there and putting enough people through them to improve economic performance would require two or three generations.
🌐 译文
幸好如此（这种观点是错误的），因为在那里建立新的教育体系并让足够多人完成学业以提高经济绩效，需要花费两三代人的时间。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干We are fortunate that it is（主系表+宾语从句）
because 从句building ... and putting ... → 两个动名词短语并列作主语 · would require → 谓语 · two or three generations → 宾语
语法动名词短语并列作主语（考研写作也可套用：Building ... and putting ... would require ...）；it is = it is wrong（省略）
词义put through 使完成（学业）· generation 代 / 代人
S05 The findings of a research institution have consistently shown that workers in all countries can be trained on the job to achieve radically higher productivity and, as a result, radically higher standards of living.
🌐 译文
一家研究机构的研究结果一直表明，所有国家的工人都可以通过在职培训获得更高的生产力，进而获得更高的生活水平。
语法分析
主干The findings ... have shown that ...（主谓+宾语从句）
宾语从句workers ... can be trained ... to achieve ... productivity and, as a result, ... standards of living
词义consistently 一贯地 · radically 根本地 / 大幅度地 · productivity 生产力
Paragraph 2 — 讽刺的是，第一个证据出现在美国
S06 Ironically, the first evidence for this idea appeared in the United States.
🌐 译文
具有讽刺意味的是，这一观点的首个证据出现在美国。
语法分析
主干the first evidence ... appeared in the United States（简单句）
词义ironically 讽刺的是（句子副词，修饰整句）· evidence 证据
S07 Not long ago, with the country entering a recession and Japan at its pre-bubble peak, the U.S. workforce was derided as poorly educated and one of the primary causes of the poor U.S. economic performance.
🌐 译文
不久前，随着美国进入衰退而日本处于泡沫前的巅峰，美国劳动力被嘲笑为受教育程度低、是美国经济表现不佳的主因之一。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干the U.S. workforce was derided as ...（被动语态 + as 补足语）
修饰with + n. + doing / prep. phrase → 独立主格结构（with the country entering... and Japan at...），作伴随状语
词义deride 嘲笑（考研词）· recession 经济衰退 · pre-bubble peak 泡沫前的顶峰
S08 Japan was, and remains, the global leader in automotive-assembly productivity.
🌐 译文
日本曾经是、现在仍然是汽车组装生产力的全球领导者。
语法分析
主干Japan was, and remains, the global leader（was 和 remains 并列谓语，时态从过去贯穿到现在）
词义automotive-assembly 汽车组装
S09 Yet the research revealed that the U.S. factories of Honda, Nissan, and Toyota achieved about 95 percent of the productivity of their Japanese counterparts—a result of the training that U.S. workers received on the job.
🌐 译文
然而研究显示，本田、日产和丰田在美国的工厂达到了其日本本土工厂生产力的约 95%——这是美国工人接受在职培训的结果。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干the research revealed that ...（主谓+宾语从句）
宾语从句the U.S. factories ... achieved about 95% of the productivity ...
修饰of their Japanese counterparts → 介词短语后置定语；破折号后 a result of the training ... → 名词短语作同位语，补充说明前面整件事；that U.S. workers received on the job → 定语从句修饰 training
词义counterpart 对应的人/物 · on the job 在职的
Paragraph 3 — 墨西哥工人的例子：不识字也能达到最高生产力
S10 More recently, while examining housing construction, the researchers discovered that illiterate, non-English-speaking Mexican workers in Houston, Texas, consistently met best-practice labor productivity standards despite the complexity of the building industry's work.
🌐 译文
更近期的研究中，在考察房屋建筑时，研究人员发现得克萨斯州休斯顿那些不识字、不会说英语的墨西哥工人，尽管建筑行业工作复杂，却始终能达到最佳实践的劳动生产率标准。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干the researchers discovered that ...（主谓+宾语从句）
修饰while examining housing construction → 省略主语的分词状语（= while they were examining）；despite the complexity ... → 让步状语
词义illiterate 不识字的 · consistently 一贯地 · best-practice 最佳实践 · complexity 复杂性
Paragraph 4 — 教育与经济发展的真正关系
S11 What is the real relationship between education and economic development?
🌐 译文
教育与经济发展之间的真正关系是什么？
语法分析
结构疑问句，主系表
S12 We have to suspect that continuing economic growth promotes the development of education even when governments don't force it.
🌐 译文
我们不得不推测，即使政府不强制推动，持续的经济增长也会促进教育的发展。
语法分析
主干We have to suspect that ...（主谓+宾语从句）
宾语从句continuing economic growth promotes ... education + even when ...（让步状语从句）
词义suspect 推测 / 认为（非"怀疑"）· promote 促进 · force 强迫
S13 After all, that's how education got started.
🌐 译文
毕竟，教育就是这样起源的。
语法分析
结构how 引导表语从句
S14 When our ancestors were hunters and gatherers 10,000 years ago, they didn't have time to wonder much about anything besides finding food.
🌐 译文
一万年前当我们的祖先还是狩猎采集者时，他们除了寻找食物外没有时间去思考其他事情。
语法分析
主干they didn't have time to wonder ...（主谓宾）
修饰When ... 10,000 years ago → 时间状语从句；besides finding food → 介词短语（besides = 除了）
词义ancestor 祖先 · hunter and gatherer 狩猎采集者
S15 Only when humanity began to get its food in a more productive way was there time for other things.
🌐 译文
只有当人类开始以更高效的方式获取食物时，才有时间去做其他事情。
语法分析 · 长难句
结构Only when ... + 倒装 → Only + 状语从句位于句首时，主句需部分倒装（was there time）
正常语序There was time for other things only when humanity began to ...
语法Only + 状语/状语从句置于句首 → 主句倒装，是考研阅读和写作高频考点
词义humanity 人类 · productive 高效的
Paragraph 5 — 教育与生产力的循环关系 + 穷国困境
S16 As education improved, humanity's productivity potential increased as well.
🌐 译文
随着教育的改善，人类的生产力潜能也提高了。
语法分析
结构As ...（时间状语从句）+ 主句
词义productivity potential 生产力潜能
S17 When the competitive environment pushed our ancestors to achieve that potential, they could in turn afford more education.
🌐 译文
当竞争环境推动祖先去实现那种潜能时，他们反过来又能负担更多的教育。
语法分析
结构When ...（时间状语从句）+ 主句
词义competitive 竞争的 · in turn 反过来 · afford 负担得起
S18 This increasingly high level of education is probably a necessary, but not a sufficient, condition for the complex political systems required by advanced economic performance.
🌐 译文
这种日益提高的教育水平，对于发达经济绩效所需的复杂政治体制来说，很可能是一个必要但不充分的条件。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干This ... level of education is ... a necessary, but not a sufficient, condition（主系表）
修饰for the complex political systems → 范围状语；required by advanced economic performance → 过去分词短语作后置定语修饰 systems
词义sufficient 充分的（与 necessary 相对）· required by 由…所要求的
S19 Thus poor countries might not be able to escape their poverty traps without political changes that may be possible only with broader formal education.
🌐 译文
因此，如果没有政治变革——而政治变革可能只有通过更广泛的正规教育才可能实现——穷国可能无法摆脱贫困陷阱。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干poor countries might not be able to escape ... without political changes（双重否定 = 条件状语）
修饰that may be possible only with broader formal education → 定语从句修饰 changes
逻辑without A, not B = 只有 A 才能 B。加上定语从句的嵌套，形成"只有更广泛的正规教育→政治变革→才能摆脱贫困"的链式推理。
词义poverty trap 贫困陷阱 · broader 更广泛的
S20 A lack of formal education, however, doesn't constrain the ability of the developing world's workforce to substantially improve productivity for the foreseeable future.
🌐 译文
然而，缺乏正规教育并不会限制发展中国家劳动力在可预见的未来大幅提高生产力的能力。
语法分析
主干A lack of formal education doesn't constrain the ability ...（主谓宾）
修饰to substantially improve productivity → 不定式后置定语修饰 ability；for the foreseeable future → 时间状语
词义constrain 约束 / 限制 · substantially 大幅度地 · foreseeable 可预见的
S21 On the contrary, constraints on improving productivity explain why education isn't developing more quickly there than it is.
🌐 译文
恰恰相反，正是对提高生产力的限制解释了为什么那里的教育没有像现在这样发展得更快。
语法分析 · 长难句
主干constraints ... explain why ...（主谓+宾语从句）
修饰on improving productivity → 介词短语后置定语修饰 constraints；why education isn't developing more quickly than it is → why 引导宾语从句，内含比较级 more quickly than
逻辑因果链条→生产力的限制→教育发展慢（而非教育限制生产力）。与 Paragraph 1 的论点呼应。
词义constraint 限制因素 · on the contrary 恰恰相反（考研写作高频连接词）

全文脉络收起

①P1: 提出论点——传统观点认为教育是穷国发展的首要任务，但作者认为这一观点是错误的（conventional view ... is wrong）。因为建新教育体系耗时两三代，而在职培训就能大幅提高生产力。
②P2: 第一个证据——讽刺性的是证据来自美国。尽管日本是汽车组装生产力霸主，但日本车企在美国的工厂达到了本土 95% 的生产力——归功于在职培训。
③P3: 第二个证据——墨西哥不识字、不会英语的工人也能达到建筑行业最佳实践标准，进一步证明正规教育并非高生产力的前提。
④P4: 因果关系反转——是经济增长促进教育，而非相反。祖先的例子（Only when ... was there time）说明：生产力提高→才有余力发展教育。
⑤P5: 循环论证——教育↔生产力互相促进，但穷国的根本问题是缺乏政治变革（而非缺教育）。缺乏正规教育并不会限制生产力提高，反倒是生产力的限制解释了教育发展缓慢的原因。

题目答案速查收起

31. The author holds in Paragraph 1 that the importance of education in poor countries ______.
A. is subject to groundless doubts B. has fallen victim of bias C. is conventionally downgraded D. has been overestimated ✓
参考答案：D

🔍 定位：P1 S03 — "the conventional view that education should be one of the very highest priorities ... is wrong"（传统观点认为教育应是最优先事项之一，但这是错的）

✅ 推理：作者认为传统观点"wrong" → 说明传统观点把教育的地位放得太高了 → 教育的重要性被高估（overestimated）。

❌ A. subject to groundless doubts（遭受无端质疑）— 原文没说教育被"质疑"，而是说它被"高估了"，方向不对。

❌ B. fallen victim of bias（成为偏见的受害者）— bias（偏见）原文未提及。

❌ C. is conventionally downgraded（被传统观念贬低了）— ❌ 方向完全相反！传统观念不是"贬低"教育，而是抬得太高（highest priorities）。

✅ D. has been overestimated（被高估了）— 对应"conventional view ... is wrong"。

32. It is stated in Paragraph 1 that the construction of a new educational system ______.
A. challenges economists and politicians B. takes efforts of generations ✓ C. demands priority from the government D. requires sufficient labor force
参考答案：B

🔍 定位：P1 S04 — "building new educational systems there ... would require two or three generations"

✅ 推理：require two or three generations = 需要两三代人的努力 → B 选项 takes efforts of generations 对应最直接。

❌ A. challenges economists and politicians — 原文说 economists and politicians 误解了关系，但没有说建立新体系对他们构成"挑战"。

✅ B. takes efforts of generations — 原文 exactly。

❌ C. demands priority from the government — 原文说 conventional view 认为教育应该 highest priority，但没说建立新体系需要政府优先处理。

❌ D. requires sufficient labor force — 劳动力原文没提。

33. A major difference between the Japanese and U.S. workforces is that ______.
A. the Japanese workforce is better disciplined B. the Japanese workforce is more productive ✓ C. the U.S. workforce has a better education D. the U.S. workforce is more organized
参考答案：B

🔍 定位：P2 S08-S09 — "Japan was, and remains, the global leader in automotive-assembly productivity" + 美国工厂只达到日本 95% 的生产力

✅ 推理：日本是全球汽车组装生产力领导者，而美国工厂只达到其 95% → 日本劳动力生产力更高。

❌ A. better disciplined（更守纪律）— 纪律性文中完全没提。

✅ B. more productive（更高效）— 正确。

❌ C. better education — ❌ 相反！文中说美国劳动力被 derided as poorly educated（教育差）。

❌ D. more organized — 组织性文中没提。

34. The author quotes the example of our ancestors to show that education emerged ______.
A. when people had enough time B. prior to better ways of finding food C. when people no longer went hungry ✓ D. as a result of pressure on government
参考答案：C

🔍 定位：P4 S14-S15 — 祖先当 hunter-gatherers 时没时间思考 → 只有用更高效方式获得食物后才"was there time for other things"（才有时间做其他事）

✅ 推理：因果关系链：生产力提高→不挨饿了→有了空闲时间→教育才得以出现。C 选项 "when people no longer went hungry"（人们不再挨饿时）准确概括了这一因果链。

❌ A. when people had enough time — 有时间只是表象，本质原因是生产力提高解决了温饱，才产生了空闲时间。A 过于表面。

❌ B. prior to better ways of finding food — ❌ 与原文相反！教育出现在寻找食物的更好方式之后。

✅ C. when people no longer went hungry — 对应的正是 "get food in a more productive way" 的结果。

❌ D. as a result of pressure on government — 政府施压文中没提。

35. According to the last paragraph, development of education ______.
A. results directly from competitive environments B. does not depend on economic performance C. follows improved productivity ✓ D. cannot afford political changes
参考答案：C

🔍 定位：P5 S20-S21 — "A lack of formal education, however, doesn't constrain the ability ... to substantially improve productivity ... On the contrary, constraints on improving productivity explain why education isn't developing more quickly"

✅ 推理：最后一段的逻辑是：不是教育限制生产力（doesn't constrain），而是生产力的限制导致了教育发展慢（constraints on productivity explain why education isn't developing）。即教育的发展追随/取决于生产力的提高 → C 正确。

❌ A. results directly from competitive environments — 竞争环境能推动 productivity potential 的实现（S17），但教育的发展直接来源于生产力提高，竞争环境是间接因素。

❌ B. does not depend on economic performance — ❌ 与原文相反！教育发展取决于经济表现（productivity）。

✅ C. follows improved productivity — 符合原文因果方向。

❌ D. cannot afford political changes — 原文说 political changes may be possible only with broader formal education（教育促进政治变革），D 说"教育负担不起政治变革"，方向反了。
